What am I looking at here?

The 3D blue sphere represents a single compartment (5.6419 μm diameter, 100 μm2 surface area) for the simulated neuron, and contains a passive leak current (reversal potential -54.3mV) and sodium and potassium active conductances.

In this practical you will run simulations of this neuron, and will be able to change a number of parameters of the model, including:

  • maximum conductance densities of ion channels
  • delay, duration and amplitude of current clamp input
  • level of holding potential of voltage clamp

and once simulations have run, you will be able to plot:

  • membrane potential
  • channel activation/inactivation variables
  • conductances of each channel
  • currents through each channel

But first, persist the model...

To be able to run simulations, the model must be persisted by pressing the icon in the toolbar (top right).

Note: you need to be registered on OSB and logged in to persist models.
