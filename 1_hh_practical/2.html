In this step we'll work with a voltage clamp instead of a current clamp. With a voltage clamp a variable current is injected into the compartment in order to keep the voltage successively at three constant levels: a conditioning level, a testing level and a return level. Before running new voltage clamp experiments start recording the current and conductance densities for the sodium and the potassium channels clicking here.
