Some tips...

 > Keep an eye on the experiment state
When the simulation is set running the status icon in the Experiments tab below will change from (pre simulation; experiment design phase), to (in the queue on our servers), to (running), and finally to (completed).

 > Hide the legend in the plots
Clicking on the button on the toolbar of individual plot windows will hide the coloured legend text (e.g. HHTutorial.hhpop[0].v).

 > Show/hide individual traces in plots
Clicking on the legend text with coloured line inside the plot will show or hide each individual trace within the plot (e.g. m, h or n).
